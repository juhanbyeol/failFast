About Pasilmo

Pasilmo, short for "Fail Fast Gathering," is an innovative platform inspired by the book "Fail Fast." We bring together 2 to 10 participants for a transformative three-week challenge designed to embrace failure as a stepping stone to success.

Start Your Journey

Key Features

Collaborative Chat Rooms

Connect with your group through real-time chat, fostering a supportive community as you navigate your challenges together.

Interactive Bulletin Board

Share insights, post updates, and celebrate milestones on our community bulletin board.

Goal Setting and Planning

Set clear objectives for 1, 2, and 3 weeks, breaking down your journey into manageable steps.

Daily Check-ins

Share your goals every morning and report on your progress each evening at 10 PM, keeping yourself accountable.

Facilitated Experience

Benefit from the guidance of a main facilitator (host) who will lead your group through the Fail Fast process.

How Pasilmo Works

  1. Form Your Group: Gather 2-10 participants who are committed to personal growth and embracing failure.
  2. Set Your Challenge: Choose a three-week period and define your collective and individual goals.
  3. Daily Engagement: Share your daily goals each morning and report on your progress every evening.
  4. Embrace Failure: Learn from setbacks, adjust your approach, and celebrate the lessons learned.
  5. Support and Grow: Engage with your group through chat and bulletin board discussions.
  6. Reflect and Evolve: At the end of three weeks, reflect on your journey and plan your next challenge.
